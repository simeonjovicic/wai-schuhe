2019
A letter from
the founder
Nils S.

I grew up between two worlds. The structured elegance of European craft and the quiet, grounded simplicity of Southeast Asia. For years I searched for a shoe that carried both — and found nothing.

Every shoe I tried felt like a compromise. Too heavy for travel. Too formal for home. Too stiff for the body to move naturally. I wanted something that disappeared — that let me feel the earth, the floor, the moment.

"I didn't set out to make a shoe. I set out to make a feeling."

WAI was born in a small workshop near Florence in 2019. Five prototypes. Eighteen months. One obsession: the perfect balance of absence and presence. A shoe you forget you're wearing, made by hands that never forget why.

This is still the first thing I check every morning — that every pair leaving our workshop carries that feeling. That promise hasn't changed.

The gesture
of wai.

In Thai culture, the wai is a bow with hands pressed together — a gesture of humility, presence, and grace. We named our brand after this philosophy: slow down, feel the ground beneath you, be present in every step.

WAI shoes are our material translation of that quiet truth. Minimal by design. Meaningful by nature.

One shoe. Every moment.
