The WAI Zero is our foundational model — a laser-cut, seamless shoe engineered to disappear. Inspired by the barefoot philosophy, it gives your foot total freedom while providing just enough structure for all-day comfort. Wear it everywhere. Notice it nowhere.

  • Upper: recycled Italian microfiber, laser-cut
  • Sole: 4mm adaptive memory foam + natural rubber base
  • Lining: breathable moisture-wicking mesh
  • Weight: 165g per shoe (size 40)
  • Foldable: yes — rolls to ⌀ 8cm

WAI Zero runs true to size. The wide toe box accommodates natural foot splay. For a more fitted feel, size down half a size. If between sizes, size up.


Available in EU 36–43. Half sizes not available.
